What is Marsi?

Marsi is a cryptocurrency that can be bought or earned in our game.Marsi is a little marshmallow who is the pilot of our rocket. In our game we invite you to join and help Marsi to get to her home planet Mars. In the game you will have to dodge asteroids and get points for every second of flight. The more time you last, the more you earn our cryptocurrency Marsi!

Access to the game

In order to start the game you have to stamp out the NFT rocket, so that Marsi can have a ship.

Project development

We plan to launch NFT Marketplace, in which anyone can create, sell and buy nft, for this you will need to have a little Marsi in your wallet to pay commissions. This will additionally keep the value of our currency in a good range. We believe in our project and would love to see YOU as a strong member of our team.
